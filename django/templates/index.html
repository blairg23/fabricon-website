GOING WHERE BRICKS-AND-MORTAR FEAR TO TREAD.

Our expertise and deep knowledge of fabrics brings your ideas to life.

For millennia, people have used fabric to cover their shelters, power their ships, clothe their bodies. WIth its light weight, high strength, and ability to conform to unusual shapes, fabric's only limitations have been those of the material itself. But thos limitations have given way to technological breakthroughs undreamed of even a few years ago.

Today's acrylics, glass, nylon, polyester, vinal, PTFE, and HDPE fabrics posses characteristics of strength, durability, stretch, and beauty that have revolutionalized the building industry. Even perennial favorites like cotton canvas perform better and last longer than ever before.

With our expertise and your imagination, the sky truly is the limit.
