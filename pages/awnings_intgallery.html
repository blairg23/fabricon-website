Awning Styles

Exterior Awnings
For less than the cost of an air-conditioning system, awnings add an attractive touch to your exterior design and cool your house without boosting your electricity bills. Awnings block harmful UV rays from entering your home, which shortens the life of carpets, drapes, and furniture. Awnings are ecologically sensible.

Interior Awnings
Using select fabrics from soft warm pastels to vibrant eye-catching patterns Fabricon awnings enhance architectural spaces. Awnings are a very flexible design medium and offer the option of unique original shapes. Interior awnings are an outstanding moment for sineage. Fabricon has full graphic capabilities and can create a total design look from start to finish.
