TENTS
Whether we're designing tents for weddings or the Antarctic our focus is on tent design that withstands the elements, locations, and situations for which they are needed. Fabricon’s standard tents include outfitter wall tents as well as large push pole designs. We custom design and manufacture many tents from a broad range of types including Geodesic, Pyramid, and Period tents.

We redesigned the Perry Geodesic for expeditions to Antarctica and constructed the military tents for use in the film Dances with Wolves.
