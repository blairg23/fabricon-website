Tipis (Teepees)
The traditional shelter for the nomadic tribes of the northern hemisphere; the conical tent, has been used globally for its ease of mobility, its sturdiness, and livability. Achieving ultimate beauty in design and construction with the Plains tribes of the West, tipis have gained the reputation as being “distinct amongst dwellings.” Fabricon makes tipis authentic to the smallest detail and built to survive the elements they were designed to withstand.

Fabricon has Tipi structures in museums across the globe including the Smithsonian institute in Washington D.C.
